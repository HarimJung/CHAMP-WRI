    What is CHAMP?

    The Coalition for High Ambition Multilevel Partnerships (CHAMP) for Climate Action is a global initiative launched at COP28 in December 2023 by the COP28 Presidency and Bloomberg Philanthropies.

    CHAMP brings together 78 endorsers — 77 countries plus the European Union — committed to enhancing cooperation between national and subnational governments to accelerate climate action.

    WRI serves as the Secretariat, coordinating efforts across regions to strengthen multilevel governance for more ambitious Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) and effective climate implementation.

    The Process

    How It Works

    01

    Consult

    National governments consult with cities, states, and regions in developing climate commitments and policies.

    02

    Collaborate

    All levels of government work together to share data, align strategies, and coordinate climate action plans.

    03

    Implement

    Endorsers implement ambitious climate policies through multilevel partnerships, ensuring local action supports national goals.
